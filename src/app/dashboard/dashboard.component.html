Electric Vehicle Analytics Dashboard

Welcome to the Electric Vehicle Analytics Dashboard! This interactive platform provides key insights into EV adoption, trends, and regional distribution. Explore the widgets below to gain a deeper understanding of the EV market and its dynamics.

Note: The graphs may take some time to load due to the large volume of data being processed. Thank you for your patience!

EV Growth by Year: Tracking Adoption Trends

This chart shows how the EV population has grown over time, categorized by EV types. It's a clear representation of market trends and adoption rates, helping us see how consumer preferences are evolving.

Regional EV Adoption: Population, Range, and Incentives

This visualization highlights the regional adoption of EVs, average vehicle ranges, and CAFV eligibility. It provides a clear picture of how EV adoption differs across locations, helping identify areas with growth potential or policy gaps.

Popular EV Makes and Models: Insights into Market Leaders

This widget explores the most popular EV manufacturers and models, along with average pricing. It's a valuable tool for understanding consumer preferences and market leaders.

Range vs. Cost: Analyzing Affordability and Efficiency

This chart correlates the electric range of vehicles with their pricing, categorized by EV type. It provides insights into how range affects affordability and highlights the positioning of different EV types in the market.
